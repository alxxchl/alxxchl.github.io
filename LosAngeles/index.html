LOS ANGELES

A vibrant coastal metropolis







OVERVIEW

Los Angeles is California’s iconic metropolis, where palm-lined boulevards meet the creative pulse of Hollywood and the Pacific coast. Known as the entertainment capital of the world, the city blends sunshine, glamour, and innovation across its sprawling neighborhoods—from the beaches of Santa Monica to the studios of Burbank. Home to over 4 million people and countless dreamers, LA thrives on diversity, ambition, and reinvention. Visitors can explore world-class museums, global cuisine, and scenic drives that stretch from desert to ocean. Best visited in spring or fall, Los Angeles embodies the spirit of possibility beneath its golden light.







INFO

Population: ~4 million (Los Angeles city), ~13 million (Greater LA area)

Language: English (with widespread Spanish usage)

Currency: US Dollar (USD, $)

Time Zone: Pacific Time (UTC −8)

Best Time to Visit: March–May (spring) & September–November (autumn)

Climate: Mediterranean; warm, dry summers and mild, wet winters
